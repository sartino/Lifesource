Greetings. With 20 years experience as a therapist, I am finding that our greatest suffering often has its deepest roots in past and present relationships. From our earliest caregivers to our adult love relationships we seek safety, intimacy and wholeness but often have been met with criticism, shame and loneliness. I work with individuals as well as with couples to address these struggles.

My approach with couples as a Certified Imago Relationship Therapist is based on the theory developed by Harville Hendrix, Ph.D., an internationally known author of the best-selling Getting the Love You Want: A Guide for Couples and Keeping the Love You Find: A Guide for Singles. Have you begun to wonder what happened to that “oneness” you felt when you fell in love with your partner? Do you feel like your partner has changed and doesn’t seem to care if s/he makes you happy any more? Are you in a power struggle? Would you like to reconnect with your partner and recommit to healing and growth as a couple?

My approach with individuals varies depending on their reason for seeking assistance. For example, sometimes we may need to address early childhood emotional wounds with a focus on healing. Or, when life-matters have become overwhelming we work to restore balance. The loss of a relationship or a job can be life-shattering and I help you to put the pieces back together. I also assist clients seeking personal growth, as well as those needing assistance with depression or anxiety. I would welcome a call from you to discuss how we can improve your life! Anne W.
